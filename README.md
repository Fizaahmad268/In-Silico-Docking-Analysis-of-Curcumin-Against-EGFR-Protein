# In-Silico-Docking-Analysis-of-Curcumin-Against-EGFR-Protein
In silico docking analysis was performed to study the interaction between Curcumin and the Epidermal Growth Factor Receptor (EGFR). The study evaluates binding affinity and interaction patterns to assess the potential of Curcumin as a natural inhibitor targeting EGFR-associated cancers.
This project performs molecular docking of Curcumin against EGFR (PDB ID: 1XKK) to evaluate binding affinity and interaction patterns.
Tools Used:
PyMOL
AutoDock Tools
AutoDock Vina
Discovery Studio
Objective:
To analyze the binding potential of Curcumin as a natural inhibitor of EGFR.
Results:
Curcumin showed favorable binding energy and stable interactions within the EGFR active site.
