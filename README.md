# In-Silico-Docking-Analysis-of-Curcumin-Against-EGFR-Protein
In silico docking analysis was performed to study the interaction between Curcumin and the Epidermal Growth Factor Receptor (EGFR). The study evaluates binding affinity and interaction patterns to assess the potential of Curcumin as a natural inhibitor targeting EGFR-associated cancers.
